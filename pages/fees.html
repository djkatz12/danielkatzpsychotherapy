75-minute group meeting: $60

Group evaluation meeting: $220

I accept self-pay and provide a superbill — a detailed receipt you can submit to your insurance for possible out-of-network reimbursement. Think of it as a way to receive partial coverage even if I’m not in-network with your plan.

I’m also in-network with several insurers,

making coverage easier for eligible clients:

  • Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Illinois
  • Aetna (Illinois and Massachusetts plans)
  • Carelon Behavioral Health (Massachusetts)
  • Quest Behavioral Health (Massachusetts)

I use a platform called Headway to manage billing and insurance services.
